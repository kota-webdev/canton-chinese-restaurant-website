City of Rams

羊城

Thousands of years ago in Guangzhou, or the “City of Rams”, was falling into a serious famine. Cantonese folklore tells the legend of 5 celestial beings that descended from above, riding rams that carried stalks of rice in their mouths to bless the people and the land. After the celestial beings returned back into the heavens, the 5 rams remained behind, turning into stone and protecting the city.
